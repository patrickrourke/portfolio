Hobbies

Music

I am a skilled saxophonist specializing in alto and soprano saxophones. I had the pleasure of performing with the Brown Jazz Band for 4 years and have played gigs with Chalumeau and Gregangelo & Velocity Circus.

Check out these performances:

  • Brown Jazz Band featuring Guillermo Klein
  • Chalumeau - "Need Me" Live at The Parlour
  • Chalumeau - HIDE Live at The Met

I am always looking to play with other musicians. If you are looking for a saxophone player to jam with or to collaborate with for a gig or other music project, please reach out!

Running and Sports

I enjoy staying active through running and team sports. Whether training for a road race, playing intramural sports with friends, or playing pickup basketball, I love the feelings of community and fulfillment that come from playing on a team and pushing myself to improve.
